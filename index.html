What we do

Discover how cloud will revolutionize your life

Hardware

Innuption is puting all of your computer's hardware onto the cloud. All you need is a WiFi connector, and a screen.

Phones

With no local hardware, our phones could be our PCs. Imagine connecting your phone to a monitor, and running instantly.

Pay as you go

As you use processing power, you'll pay a fee. Much less than if you had bought hardware for yourself.

Blockchain

DApps. Users can sell their PC's processing power directly to other users. No middleman, lower prices.
